About Me

I am a graduate student at Jilin University. My research interests include autonomous driving, intelligent vehicles, multimodal BEV perception, 3D scene understanding, occupancy prediction, driving world models, vehicle dynamics, path tracking control, and robust chassis control.

My current work focuses on building reliable autonomous driving systems by combining perception, prediction, and control. In particular, I am interested in camera-LiDAR fusion, bird's-eye-view representation learning, occupancy-based scene modeling, and future scene evolution prediction for complex driving environments.
